Welcome to the Ministry of Law & Justice

Our Mission:

our mission is to empower undertrial prisoners with innovative tech solutions. We are dedicated to providing equitable access to legal resources, information, and support, ensuring that the rights and dignity of every undertrial prisoner are upheld. Through transparency, efficiency, and collaboration, we strive to create a system that values rehabilitation, safeguards human rights, and paves the way for successful reintegration into society.

Services:

We are responsible for courts, prisons, probation services and attendance centres.
